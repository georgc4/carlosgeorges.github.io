Privacy Policy

Recast — Dice-Word Puzzle Game • Effective March 2, 2026

Overview

Recast is an offline puzzle game. Your privacy is straightforward: we don't collect your data.

Data Collection

Recast does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No accounts or sign-ups are required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No advertising networks are used
  • No cookies or device identifiers are collected
  • No data is sent to any server

Local Storage

All game data — including your scores, statistics, saved games, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Recast does not integrate any third-party services, analytics providers, or ad networks.

Children's Privacy

Recast does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
